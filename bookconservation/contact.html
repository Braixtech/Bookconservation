Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can apply to volunteer through our Volunteer Portal. Create an account, complete your profile, and browse current opportunities. Most positions require an application and brief interview process. Visit our Volunteer page for more details.

Public research materials are available in our Digital Library. For access to restricted datasets or specialized research materials, you'll need to register as a researcher. Visit our Research Portal for registration information and access guidelines.

Yes, we accept donations of historical materials relevant to Northern Nigeria's heritage. Please contact our collections team at collections@arewaconservation.org with details about the materials. All donations go through a review process to ensure they align with our conservation mission.

For technical issues with the website or platform, please email support@arewaconservation.org with details of the issue, including screenshots if possible, and information about your device and browser. Our support team typically responds within 24-48 hours.
