Preserving Northern Nigeria's Heritage

Dedicated to conserving the cultural and natural heritage of the Arewa region

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles for conservation efforts

Our Mission

To preserve, document, and promote the rich cultural heritage and biodiversity of Northern Nigeria through innovative conservation strategies, community engagement, and digital preservation.

Our Vision

To become the leading digital conservation platform in West Africa, creating a sustainable future where cultural heritage and biodiversity thrive together.

Our Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Sustainability, and Respect for indigenous knowledge and traditions.

Our Story

Journey through our conservation timeline

2010

Foundation

Founded by a group of heritage enthusiasts and conservationists concerned about the rapid loss of cultural artifacts in Northern Nigeria.

2015

Digital Transformation

Launched our first digital catalog with 500 documented artifacts and heritage sites.

2020

Platform Launch

Officially launched the Arewa Conservation Platform, integrating digital preservation with community engagement.

2023

Expansion

Expanded to cover 12 Northern states with over 10,000 documented heritage items and 50 active conservation projects.
